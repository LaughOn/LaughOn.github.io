How to use LibreOffice to Download a Spreadsheet!

LibreOffice has many features, such as its ability to let you create spreadsheets!

Context

This will allow you to use LibreOffice for many tasks!

Pre-requisites

  • Have LibreOffice downloaded

Steps

  1. Open LibreOffice
  2. Click Calc Spreadsheet, under Create:
  3. Use the program as you would Microsoft Excel!
